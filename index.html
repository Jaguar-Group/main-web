[ 02 — Music Duel ]

Songbattles

Two songs. One winner. We pull two random tracks from the current charts and make you choose. It's like Tinder for music, except you actually feel guilty about your choices.

STATUS Live
FEATURE Live Chart Integration, 1v1 Mode
PLAY Link coming soon ↗
How it works
  1. 01 The engine pulls two random songs from the current top charts.
  2. 02 You listen to both (or just skip to the chorus, we won't judge).
  3. 03 You vote. The winner moves up, the loser gets replaced.
[ 03 — Build Challenge ]

Trailmakers Challenge Generator

For when you and your friends look at a sandbox game and say, "What now?" The TCG spits out a random build time, an evaluation criteria, a goal, and a special rule.

Example: 15 minutes, build a submarine, evaluation criteria is combat, special rule: it must be able to drive on land.
